This is the series that started it all!
Very underrated when it was originally released, but had a tremendous increase in popularity years later. Six spinoffs later, and several movies, Star Trek remains one of the most popular entertainment franchises in history.

The crew of the space station known as Deep Space Nine didn't travel where no one has gone before. At least not in a ship, like the other series. The first stable wormhole in existance was discovered nearby, though, which brought all kinds of new situations, including enemies from the Gamma Quadrant. Much of this series dealt with this conflict.

Rather than traveling away from Earth and its neighbors in space, this unfortunate crew spent its journey trying to get back home, after being stranded on the far side of the Milky Way galaxy in its first episode. This allowed them to discover lots of species and anomalies that none of the other series had. This was the first series with a female captain.
